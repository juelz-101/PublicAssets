From The Block To The Top

Various Artists

From The Block To The Top is a raw and authentic collection of old-school rap and hip-hop, showcasing the talent of underground artists from across America. This album captures the gritty essence of life on the streets and the unstoppable drive to rise above it, delivering hard-hitting beats, razor-sharp lyrics, and unfiltered stories of hustle and ambition. From concrete jungles to dreams of the big stage, every track is a tribute to the culture, creativity, and resilience that define hip-hop. A must-listen for fans of classic vibes and real storytelling.
