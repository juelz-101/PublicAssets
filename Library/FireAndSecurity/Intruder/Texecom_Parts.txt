# Texecom Intruder Alarm Parts List

# Common Part IDs for Premier Elite and Ricochet Wireless Series

## Control Panels (Premier Elite)

**CAC-0001** | Premier Elite 24 (Polymer Cabinet)
> 

Grade 2 entry-level panel. Comes in a plastic (polymer) housing. Onboard 8 zones, expandable to 24. Ideal for residential.

**CAA-0026** | Premier Elite 48 (Metal Cabinet)
> 

The standard installer choice. Grade 3 metal panel. 8 zones onboard, expandable to 48. Room for a 7Ah battery and expanders inside.

**CAB-0001** | Premier Elite 88 (Metal Cabinet)
> 

Mid-range commercial panel. Expandable to 88 zones. Grade 3. Used when the 48 just isn't quite big enough.

**CAC-0002** | Premier Elite 168 (Metal Cabinet)
> 

High-spec commercial panel. Expandable to 168 zones. Supports larger power supplies and more complex cause-and-effect programming.

**CAD-0001** | Premier Elite 640 (Large Metal Cabinet)
> 

Flagship panel. Expandable to 640 zones. Comes in a massive enclosure to house multiple expanders or power supplies. Used in large schools/hospitals.

## Keypads

**DBC-0001** | Premier Elite LCDP
> 

Standard small-screen keypad with built-in proximity tag reader. White finish with blue backlighting.

**DBB-0001** | Premier Elite LCDLP
> 

Large screen keypad with proximity reader. Features a much larger display for easier programming and custom text. The "Installer's Favourite".

**DBE-0001** | Premier Elite FMK (Flush Mount)
> 

Flush-mount keypad for a sleek finish. Requires a backbox cut into the wall. Premium aesthetic.

**JAA-0001** | Premier LCDL
> 

Older style "Green Screen" large keypad. No proximity reader. Often found on legacy installs or budget specs.

## Expansion & Power

**CCB-0001** | Premier Elite 8XP
> 

Wired zone expander. Adds 8 hardwired zones to the system. Connected via the 4-core data bus.

**CCF-0001** | Premier Elite 60iXD
> 

Intelligent Power Supply and Expander combined. Provides 2.5A power and 8 zones. Monitored by the panel for faults.

**ACD-0001** | Premier Elite 60iXD (PCB Only)
> 

Replacement PCB for the 60iXD. Useful for service calls where the box is fine but the PSU has failed.

**FCE-0001** | PSU200
> 

Standalone 2.5A Power Supply. "Dumb" unit (uses relay outputs to signal faults to the panel).

**HAC-0001** | Premier Elite OP16
> 

Output module giving 16 programmable outputs. Used for driving LEDs, relays, or automation interfacing.

## Wireless (Ricochet Mesh Technology)

**GCD-0001** | Premier Elite 32XP-W
> 

Wireless Expander. Adds 32 wireless zones using Ricochet Mesh (devices talk to each other to repeat signals).

**GCE-0001** | Premier Elite 8XP-W
> 

Smaller wireless expander. Adds 8 wireless zones. Good for hybrid systems needing just a few wireless detectors.

**GCC-0001** | Premier Elite SmartKey
> 

Two-way wireless keyfob. Can show system status (Arm/Disarm) via LEDs.

**RMS-0001** | Ricochet Monitor Tool (MT2)
> 

Essential engineer tool. A handheld device that checks signal strength and mesh paths for Ricochet sensors.

**GHA-0001** | Micro Contact-W
> 

Tiny, discreet wireless door contact. Fits inside many door frames or looks unobtrusive on the surface.

**GDA-0001** | Impaq Contact-W
> 

Standard size wireless contact with inputs for external wired sensors (e.g., wired roller shutter contact).

## Motion Detectors (Capture Series)

**AKC-0001** | Capture P15
> 

Wired PIR. 15m range. Grade 2. The standard wired detector replacing the old Prestige IR.

**AKD-0001** | Capture D20
> 

Wired Dual Tech (PIR + Microwave). 20m range. Grade 3. Anti-masking active.

**GDA-0001** | Capture P15-W
> 

Wireless version of the P15. Ricochet enabled.

**GDB-0001** | Capture D20-W
> 

Wireless Dual Tech. Ricochet enabled. Ideal for garages or conservatories where standard PIRs might false alarm.

**GDC-0001** | Capture CQ-W
> 

Wireless Quad Element PIR. Better false alarm immunity than the P15-W but not quite a Dual Tech.

## Sounders (Odyssey X Series)

_Note: Covers and Backplates are sold separately._

**WDA-0001** | Odyssey X-BE Backplate
> 

Grade 2 Backplate (The electronics and piezo). Wireless/Wired hybrid ready.

**WDB-0001** | Odyssey X-B Backplate
> 

Grade 3 Backplate. Louder sounder, better battery backup monitoring.

**WDC-0002** | Odyssey X1 Cover
> 

Rectangular/Vertical cover. Available in White or Black with Blue/Red lenses.

**WDD-0002** | Odyssey X3 Cover
> 

Hexagonal cover (Classic "Bell" shape). Most common visual deterrent.

## Communicators & App Access

**CEL-0001** | Premier Elite SmartCom
> 

The modern communicator. Connects via Ethernet (and WiFi on newer models) to the Texecom Cloud for App control.

**CEC-0001** | Premier Elite ComIP
> 

Legacy Ethernet module. Allows polling to an ARC but does not support the Connect App natively without upgrades.

**CFC-0001** | Premier Elite ComGSM
> 

2G GSM module for SMS text alerts or backup signalling.

**CED-0001** | Speech Dialler
> 

Standalone module that connects to a phone line. Plays a recorded voice message to user mobiles upon alarm.

## Accessories

**JAB-0001** | Premier Elite USB Com
> 

The "PC-Com" cable. Connects a laptop running Wintex software to the panel for programming.

**JAC-0001** | Premier Elite PC-Com (Serial)
> 

Serial (DB9) version of the PC connection cable. Often more reliable for firmware flashing than the USB version.

**PREMTAG** | Proximity Tags
> 

Standard Texecom teardrop tags. Pack of 5. Robust and reliable.
