# Honeywell Galaxy Intruder Alarm Parts List

# Common Part IDs for Galaxy Dimension, Flex, and G2 Series

## Control Panels (Dimension Series)

**C048-D-E1** | Galaxy Dimension GD-48 Control Panel
> 

Entry-level commercial panel. Supports up to 48 zones. Grade 3 capable. Popular in small-to-medium retail.

**C096-D-E1** | Galaxy Dimension GD-96 Control Panel
> 

Mid-range commercial panel. Supports up to 96 zones. ideal for larger retail or small office buildings.

**C264-D-E1** | Galaxy Dimension GD-264 Control Panel
> 

High-end commercial panel. Supports up to 264 zones. Standard for large warehouses and logistics centres.

**C520-D-E1** | Galaxy Dimension GD-520 Control Panel
> 

Maximum capacity panel. Supports up to 520 zones and 32 groups. Used in massive industrial sites or airports.

## Control Panels (Flex Series)

**C005-M-E2** | Galaxy Flex FX020 Panel (Medium Box)
> 

Hybrid panel with 12 onboard zones, expandable to 20. Fits in a medium poly box. Grade 2. Great for residential/small business.

**C006-L-E2** | Galaxy Flex FX050 Panel (Large Box)
> 

Hybrid panel expandable to 50 zones. Large poly box allows space for expanders/battery. Grade 2.

**C007-L-E2** | Galaxy Flex FX100 Panel (Large Box)
> 

Top-tier Flex panel, expandable to 100 zones. Grade 2 (can be Grade 3 with metal kit). Bridges the gap between Flex and Dimension.

## Keypads & Interfaces

**CP037-00** | MK7 LCD Keypad
> 

The industry standard keypad. 2x16 character display with rubber keys. Retro style but bulletproof reliability.

**CP038-01** | MK7 Keyprox
> 

Identical to the MK7 but with an integrated proximity reader (ASK format) for setting/unsetting with fobs.

**CP050-00-01** | MK8 LCD Keypad
> 

Modern aesthetic keypad with a cleaner look and harder plastic keys. Functionally identical to MK7.

**CP051-00-01** | MK8 Keyprox
> 

The modern MK8 keypad with integrated proximity reader.

**CP041-00** | TouchCenter Plus
> 

Full-colour touchscreen keypad. Great for end-users who want a tablet-like interface. Can show zone plans/graphics.

## Expansion Modules (RIOs) & Power

**C072** | Galaxy RIO (Remote Input/Output)
> 

The "bread and butter" expander. Adds 8 hardwired zones and 4 programmable transistor outputs to the system. RS485 bus connected.

**P026-01-B** | Power RIO (Boxed)
> 

Combined 2.75A Power Supply and RIO (8 zones/4 outputs) in a metal cabinet. Essential for powering distant expanders.

**P025-01-B** | Smart PSU (Boxed)
> 

Intelligent 2.75A Power Supply. Monitored by the panel (AC fail, Battery, Fuse). No zones onboard.

**A158-B** | Galaxy Flex RIO
> 

A specific RIO PCB designed to clip directly inside the Galaxy Flex polymer enclosure to save space.

## Communication Modules

**E080-10** | Ethernet Module (New V4)
> 

The latest Ethernet module for Dimension and Flex. Essential for reliable app connectivity and signalling.

**A083-00-01** | Ethernet Module (E080-V2)
> 

Older variant of the Ethernet module. Still common but E080-10 is preferred for new installs.

**A081-00-01** | GSM/GPRS Module
> 

Provides mobile network backup. Can send SMS or signal to ARC via GPRS if the landline/network fails.

**A228** | RS232 Interface Module
> 

Used for integrating the alarm with third-party systems (Crestron, PC Automation) or local PC connection.

**A229** | Galaxy Trigger Lead
> 

Simple header cable that plugs into the panel to provide hardwired trigger outputs (Fire, PA, Intruder) for legacy diallers.

## Access Control & Wireless

**C080-00** | DCM (Door Control Module)
> 

Fully integrated access control module. Controls 2 doors (Read in/out or Read in/Button out). Uses RS485 bus.

**C079-2** | RF Portal (Standalone)
> 

Wireless receiver in its own plastic housing. Wired to the RS485 bus. Allows use of V2 wireless sensors.

**A073-00-01** | RF Portal (Flex PCB)
> 

Wireless receiver PCB that clips directly inside a Flex panel housing. No external box needed.

## Common Wired Sensors

**IS312** | Honeywell Wired PIR
> 

Standard grade 2 PIR. Features a switchable pet-immune lens. Low cost, reliable.

**DT7550** | Dual Tech Sensor
> 

Commercial Grade 3 Dual Technology (PIR + Microwave) sensor. Anti-masking features. Used in high-risk areas.

**Vibrasensor** | Viper Shock Sensor
> 

The classic white shock sensor. Adjustable sensitivity. Requires a separate analyser if not using the newer "calibration" versions.

## Accessories & Tools

**A234** | Galaxy RS485 Data Lead
> 

Specialist cable for flashing firmware or connecting a laptop directly to the RS485 bus.

**YCIT01** | MAX4 Proximity Card
> 

Standard credit-card style proximity tag for Galaxy systems.

**YX0-0020** | Keyprox Fob
> 

Tough black plastic teardrop fob. The standard user token for all Galaxy Keyprox units.

**SPI-KEY** | SPI Programmer Key
> 

Small memory stick tool that plugs into the panel PCB. Used to save a copy of the programming or clone it to another panel.
