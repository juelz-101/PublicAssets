2. Developer Mode

Open settings, scroll all the way to bottom and select "About Phone"
Scroll to Software Infomation.

Tap on One UI Version 8 times to turn on Developer Mode. 

Turn it on and return to main settings window, Search "Mock" and scroll to bottom to select "Select Mock Location App".

Select your chosen GPS Emulator. 

---

3. Select Location

Exit back to the homescreen and find your chosen GPS Emulator app.
Open it and Set location to your chosen location.
Apply/Confirm your location and you are done.

4. Confirm Its Working

Easiest way is to open up Maps, if it shows you are at your chosen location then well done.
Mess around with the options in your GPS Emulator to see what you can do.
As stated our goal was just to set a custom location, so I just recommeded the first free app that worked for me.

Notes:
