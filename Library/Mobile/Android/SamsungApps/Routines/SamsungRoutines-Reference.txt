# Samsung Modes and Routines Reference

This guide lists the available **Conditions** (triggers) and **Actions** (results) for creating automated routines on Samsung One UI devices.

# Part 1: Conditions (IF)

_These triggers start the routine when detected._

## Connected devices
- 

**Smart View:** Triggers when your phone is casting to a TV or screen.
- 

**Multi control:** Triggers when connected to a Galaxy Book or Tablet via Multi Control.
- 

**Link to Windows:** Triggers when the phone is connected to your PC.

## Device
- 

**Wired headphones:** Detects when headphones are plugged in or unplugged.
- 

**Charging status:** specific triggers for Wired charging, Wireless charging, or Not charging.
- 

**Battery level:** Triggers when battery hits a specific percentage (e.g., "Below 20%" or "Equal to or above 80%").
- 

**Power saving:** Detects if Power Saving mode is currently On or Off.
- 

**Sound mode:** Triggers based on the current mode (Sound, Vibrate, or Mute).
- 

**Dark mode:** Detects if the system Dark Mode is active.
- 

**Watch wearing status:** Detects if you are currently wearing your Galaxy Watch (requires paired watch).

## Event
- 

**Game is being played:** Automatically triggers when a game app launches.
- 

**During exercise:** Triggers when a workout is manually started or auto-detected in Samsung Health.
- 

**Media is being played:** Detects when music or video is playing from any app.

## Call
- 

**Incoming call:** Triggers when the phone rings (can specify "from anyone" or "specific contacts").
- 

**During call:** Triggers while you are actively on a phone call.

# Part 2: Actions (THEN)

_These changes happen automatically when the conditions above are met._

## Accessibility
- 

**Amplify ambient sound:** Boosts external volume through headphones (similar to hearing aid functionality).
- 

**Assistant menu:** Toggles the floating on-screen button for quick navigation access.
- 

**Camera flash notification:** Flashes the camera light when you receive alerts.
- 

**Colour correction:** Adjusts colour spectrums for users with colour blindness.
- 

**Colour filter:** Applies a coloured overlay to the screen (e.g., blue light filter).
- 

**Colour inversion:** Inverts all colours on the screen (white becomes black, etc.).
- 

**Extra dim:** Lowers the screen brightness beyond the standard minimum setting.
- 

**Left/right sound balance:** Adjusts volume balance between left and right audio channels.
- 

**Live Caption:** Automatically detects speech in media and displays captions.
- 

**Mono audio:** Combines left and right audio channels into a single mono signal.
- 

**Mute all sounds:** Silences the entire device instantly.
- 

**Screen flash notification:** Flashes the screen display when notifications arrive.

## Accounts and backup
- 

**Auto sync data:** Toggles whether accounts (Google, Samsung, etc.) automatically sync in the background.

## Alarm
- 

**Turn off alarms:** Disables currently active alarms.
- 

**Turn on alarms:** Enables specific alarms.

## Apps
- 

**Close an app:** Force closes a specific application.
- 

**Open an app pair:** Launches two specific apps simultaneously in split-screen mode.

## Battery
- 

**Battery protection:** Limits maximum charge (usually to 80% or 85%) to extend battery lifespan.
- 

**Fast charging:** Toggles wired fast charging capabilities.
- 

**Fast wireless charging:** Toggles high-speed wireless charging.

## Bixby
- 

**Ask Bixby:** Triggers the voice assistant to listen for a command.

## Connected devices
- 

**Call & text on other devices:** Allows calls/texts to be routed to your Galaxy Tab or Watch.
- 

**Link to Windows:** Connects the phone to a PC via the Phone Link app.
- 

**Multi control:** Allows a Galaxy Book mouse/keyboard to control the phone.
- 

**Quick Share:** Adjusts visibility for file sharing with nearby devices.
- 

**Samsung DeX:** Launches the desktop-like interface on a connected monitor.

## Connections
- 

**Bluetooth:** Turns Bluetooth on or off.
- 

**Connect to a Bluetooth device:** Connects to a _specific_ saved device (e.g., headphones).
- 

**Connect to a VPN:** Initiates a connection to a specific Virtual Private Network.
- 

**Data saver:** Restricts background data usage for apps.
- 

**Disconnect from a Bluetooth device:** Disconnects a specific device.
- 

**Disconnect from a VPN:** Ends the VPN session.
- 

**Flight mode:** Toggles cellular, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth radios off.
- 

**Mobile Hotspot:** Turns your phone into a Wi-Fi router.
- 

**Mobile data:** Toggles 4G/5G cellular data.
- 

**Music Share:** Allows friends to play music on your Bluetooth speaker via your phone.
- 

**NFC:** Toggles Near Field Communication (for payments/tags).
- 

**Network mode:** Switches between network types (e.g., LTE only, 5G preferred).
- 

**Show Wi-Fi QR code:** Displays the QR code for others to join your current network.
- 

**Wi-Fi:** Turns Wi-Fi on or off.

## Device care
- 

**Optimise phone:** Clears background apps and RAM to boost performance.
- 

**Performance profile:** Switches between Standard (faster) and Light (battery saving) performance profiles.

## Display
- 

**Accidental touch protection:** Prevents pocket dials when the screen is dark.
- 

**Adaptive brightness:** Toggles auto-brightness based on ambient light.
- 

**Adaptive colour tone:** Adjusts screen colour temperature based on ambient light (True Tone).
- 

**Brightness:** Sets the screen brightness to a specific fixed percentage.
- 

**Edge panels:** Toggles the slide-out side menu.
- 

**Font size:** Changes text size.
- 

**Font style:** Changes the system font family.
- 

**Motion smoothness:** Toggles refresh rate (e.g., 60Hz vs 120Hz).
- 

**Screen orientation:** Locks or unlocks portrait/landscape rotation.
- 

**Screen resolution:** Changes display sharpness (e.g., FHD+ vs WQHD+).
- 

**Screen timeout:** Sets how long the screen stays on before sleeping.
- 

**Screen zoom:** Adjusts the scaling of UI elements.
- 

**Touch sensitivity:** Increases sensitivity (useful when using screen protectors).

## Effects
- 

**Beep once:** Plays a single system beep.
- 

**Edge lighting effect once:** Flashes the edge of the screen with a specific colour.
- 

**Vibrate once:** Triggers a short haptic vibration.

## Functions
- 

**Go to website:** Opens a specific URL in the default browser.
- 

**Navigation button actions:** Modifies what the back/home/recent keys do.
- 

**Open Air command with Pen button:** (S-Pen devices) Toggles S-Pen menu behavior.
- 

**Read text aloud:** Text-to-speech for a specific phrase.
- 

**Record screen:** Starts a screen recording.
- 

**Take screenshot:** Captures the current screen.
- 

**Torch:** Turns the flashlight on or off.
- 

**Video brightness:** Enhances screen brightness specifically when watching video apps.

## Galaxy Watch

_(Controls for a paired watch)_
- 

**Always On Display:** Toggles the watch face staying on.
- 

**Brightness:** Sets watch screen brightness.
- 

**Disconnection alerts:** Vibrates when the watch loses phone connection.
- 

**Power saving:** Toggles watch battery saver mode.
- 

**Raise wrist to wake:** Toggles waking the watch screen via gesture.
- 

**Sound mode:** Switches watch between Sound, Vibrate, or Mute.
- 

**Touch screen to wake:** Toggles waking watch by tapping.
- 

**Turn bezel to wake:** Toggles waking watch by rotating the bezel.

## Gallery
- 

**Show images:** Opens a specific picture or album.

## Game Booster
- 

**Block Bixby during games:** Prevents voice assistant interruptions.
- 

**Block auto brightness during games:** Locks brightness while gaming.
- 

**Block edge panel during games:** Disables the side swipe menu while gaming.

## Location
- 

**Location:** Toggles GPS services on or off.

## Lock screen and AOD
- 

**Always On Display:** Toggles the phone's standby info screen.
- 

**Keep your phone unlocked:** Extends Smart Lock duration.
- 

**Unlock with biometrics:** Automatically attempts to unlock using face/fingerprint.

## Music
- 

**Spotify playlists:** Starts playing a specific Spotify playlist.

## Notifications
- 

**Show custom notification:** Displays a text notification you write yourself (useful for reminders).

## Phone
- 

**Answer using speaker:** Automatically answers incoming calls on speakerphone.
- 

**Decline call and send a quick message:** Rejects call and texts the caller.

## Samsung Keyboard
- 

**High contrast keyboard:** Changes keyboard colours for visibility.
- 

**Languages and types:** Switches input languages.
- 

**Mode:** Changes keyboard layout (standard, split, floating).
- 

**Samsung Keyboard voice input:** Toggles voice-to-text.

## Security
- 

**Turn on Secure Wi-Fi:** Enables Samsung's privacy protection for public networks.

## Sounds and vibration
- 

**AI assistant volume:** Sets volume for Bixby/Google.
- 

**Boost dialogue:** Enhances vocal clarity in audio.
- 

**Call volume:** Sets volume for phone calls.
- 

**Dialing keypad tone:** Toggles the sound made when typing numbers.
- 

**Dolby Atmos:** Toggles surround sound processing.
- 

**Equaliser:** Applies a specific audio EQ preset (e.g., Rock, Pop).
- 

**Loudness normalisation:** Keeps volume consistent across tracks.
- 

**Ringtone:** Changes the incoming call ringtone.
- 

**Samsung Keyboard sound:** Toggles typing sounds.
- 

**Samsung Keyboard vibration:** Toggles typing haptics.
- 

**Separate app sound:** Routes specific app audio to a different device (e.g., Spotify to speaker, Nav to phone).
- 

**Vibrate while ringing:** Toggles vibration during calls.

## Timer
- 

**Start a timer:** Begins a countdown for a set duration.

## Wallpaper
- 

**Dim wallpaper when Dark mode is on:** Darkens the background image to match the system theme.
